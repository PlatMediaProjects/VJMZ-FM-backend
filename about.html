About Napoleon S. Brewer Sr.

Known professionally by the single-name moniker Sha'Mon, Napoleon S. Brewer Sr. is a multifaceted talent in the world of music, business, and philanthropy. As a singer-songwriter, entertainer, concert promoter, and visionary, Sha'Mon has devoted his life to creating platforms that uplift, inspire, and open doors of opportunity for others.

He is the President and CEO of Platinum Hits Entertainment Inc., a company dedicated to artist development and music production, and the founder of two transformative organizations: TPL Endowment Foundation Inc. and First One Consultants, Inc. These companies, along with their many trade names, brands, and subsidiaries, represent his passion for business innovation and community empowerment.

Through his work, Napoleon has built more than just businesses — he has built bridges. He believes deeply in creating environments where others can grow, launch, and thrive. His efforts are not about recognition, but about legacy — about doing what is right, not just what is profitable.

VJMZ-FM.com, Internet's #1 JAM'N STATION, is an extension of that mission. It is not just a station — it’s a platform, a voice, a listening ear for those who wish to be heard. Through music, conversation, and cultural celebration, Napoleon invites listeners, artists, and entrepreneurs to join him in creating a world where creativity and opportunity walk hand-in-hand.

With humility and honor, he shares this station and all he builds with the world — not to boast, but to build.
